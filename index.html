How to Play

  • You're Waddle Dee competing against Kirby for a burger!
  • Click "Roll Dice" for 3 rounds of dice rolls. The highest total wins the burger.
  • If a dice shows 1, round score resets to 0. If both dice match, the score doubles. Otherwise, the score is the sum of dice values.
  • In case of a tie, another round is played. Click "New Game" to restart.
